WIM | WHAT IS MY MAJOR?

WIMM stands for “What Is My Major?” is a website which is mainly suitable for Students who are going to graduate from high school or who are looking for a major, particularly students who struggle to choose a major or are hesitant about the major that they are going to choose. Moreover, our website will assist students in their college decision to find their preferred/suitable major by assigning majors based on the responses to our queries.


WIM for WHO?

WIM is for EVERYONE! Are you looking for major? Are you stuck in deciding your education path? WIM is for you. As mentioned above, WIM can help you make decision on pursuing your education in the university. WIM website will principally use the AUPP majors in order to reduce the scope of designing the algorithm that can recommend all majors in Cambodia instead we limit it to the majors available at AUPP including Business Administration, Global Affairs, Information Technology Management and Computer Science, Information and Communication Technology, Law and lastly Tourism and Hospitality Management and in a way to promote AUPP as well. Students will go through several questions and choose answers based on what and how they are interested.


Why WIM?

WIMM will help to determine and recommend an appropriate major. As a result, students will be given the most appropriate major to help them decide whether or not to pursue it. However, for those students who decided their major already but insufficiently, the website will also fulfill their missing space to finalize their appropriate major. Following this, there will be criteria answers that convert the students' responses and output their suitable major for them at the end of the query. Therefore, we will recommend AUPP majors at the end of their quiz, along with a description of the major, course information, and other relevant information.


Who behind WIM?

WIMM website is creditably contributed by Chap Chanmanith and Yeang Lyhour who established this website by developed this website from scratch. Additionally, the website is originally designed by our own concept and mixed enterprise after a several discussions of established this idea. After the completion of this project, we are hoping to continue maintain this website and publish it for the public uses especially Cambodian's students (which will hopefully available in Khmer Language). We are hoping that this website would be a small factor in the decision of pursuing Bachelor Degree education for all high school students.
