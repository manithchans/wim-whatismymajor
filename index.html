WIM | WHAT IS MY MAJOR?

WIM? stands for “What Is My Major?” is a website which is mainly suitable for Students who are going to graduate from high school or who are looking for a major, particularly students who struggle to choose a major or are hesitant about the major that they are going to choose. Moreover, our website will assist students in their college decision to find their preferred/suitable major by assigning majors based on the responses to our queries.


WIM for WHO?

WIM is for EVERYONE! Are you looking for major? Are you stuck in deciding your education path? WIM is for you. As mentioned above, WIM can help you make decision on pursuing your education in the university. WIM website will principally use the AUPP majors in order to reduce the scope of designing the algorithm that can recommend all majors in Cambodia instead we limit it to the majors available at AUPP including Business Administration, Global Affairs, Information Technology Management and Computer Science, Information and Communication Technology, Law and lastly Tourism and Hospitality Management and in a way to promote AUPP as well. Students will go through several questions and choose answers based on what and how they are interested.


Why WIM?

WIM will help to determine and recommend an appropriate major. As a result, students will be given the most appropriate major to help them decide whether or not to pursue it. However, for those students who decided their major already but insufficiently, the website will also fulfill their missing space to finalize their appropriate major. Following this, there will be criteria answers that convert the students' responses and output their suitable major for them at the end of the query. Therefore, we will recommend AUPP majors at the end of their quiz, along with a description of the major, course information, and other relevant information.


Who behind WIM?

WIM website is creditably contributed by Chap Chanmanith and Yeang Lyhour who established this website by developed this website from scratch. Additionally, the website is originally designed by our own concept and mixed enterprise after a several discussions of established this idea. After the completion of this project, we are hoping to continue maintain this website and publish it for the public uses especially Cambodian's students (which will hopefully available in Khmer Language). We are hoping that this website would be a small factor in the decision of pursuing Bachelor Degree education for all high school students.

HOW TO USE WIM?

1. To begin with, when the website is fully loaded, you can find the button named “FIND MY MAJOR” in the middle of the website and the lower part of the website, there are 6 buttons which are the majors where you can click on those buttons to read the information of each major.

2. To start the quiz, firstly, click on the button which later on the questions will be shown. There are 15 questions in total which there will be between 4 and 6 answers in each question.

3. After reading the question carefully, you can click on one answer below the question and click the arrow button on the lower right corner to go to the next question.

4. After completing the 15 questions, your chosen answer will be generated and your future major will pop up as well in a few seconds. You can read the following information which is mainly related to your major after the quiz.

5. If you want to redo the quiz, you can click the home button and follow the same instructions above.

Note: The result is NOT 100% accurate, we analyzed your input based upon the research on major and personality, students' learning experiences, and the survey. We are hoping that WIM can ease your difficulty in finding your college major. Please seek guidance from your family, friends, and especially yourselves. Your Major, Your Choice, Your Future!

If you have any concerns or suggestions, please contact to us via the information located in "Project Info | WIM 2022". Thanks!
